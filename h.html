About MBM

My Fitness Journey

For years I felt insecure about being skinny, I wanted the hourglass figure, the curves, the butt and hips. After lots of trial and error, I discovered the transformative power of proper training and nutrition. I had to find what works best for me, once I learned about what type of body I had, I was able to reach my goals by working around it. My journey from frustration to fitness inspired me to help others achieve their goals.

With over 8 years of experience in the fitness industry, I've developed proven methods to help clients of all fitness levels build strength, lose fat, and gain confidence.

What Sets MBM Apart

At MUSCLESBYMIK, we don't believe in one-size-fits-all solutions. Every program is tailored to your unique body type, goals, and lifestyle. We focus on sustainable results through science-backed training and nutrition strategies.
